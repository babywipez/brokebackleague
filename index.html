Welcome to the 2022 Season!

This website is wholly dedicated to the greatest fantasy football league in the universe.

A wise man once said, "A fantasy football league is only as good as its players - each providing their own form of passive harassment. Some pick up guys like McKissic and get 30 points in PPR while others invest in Saquon in the second and lose the week because he only put up 8."-Anonymous

I agree with anonymous. The people, the shit talking, the devastating losses, the frustrating waiver wire pickups, they all come together yearly in beautiful culmination of competitive dick swinging.

So, I've decided to honor our yearly gathering with a website. I've also written and uploaded the first article. If you would like to write for the website, or if you have ideas for stuff to add to it, PLEASE let me know! I'll do my best to keep this updated and fun throughout the year.

And, so I say to you, fellow competitors: Cheers! A league is only as good as its people. Here's to you! Let's make this year awesome!

-Chris
